## 📋 Step-by-Step: Create Facebook Page

### Step 1: Create the Page (2 minutes)

1. **Go to:** https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
2. **Click:** "Get Started"
3. **Choose:** "Cause or Community" (or "Non-Profit Organization")
4. **Page Name:** League of Women Voters of Newport County
5. **Category:** Type "Non-Profit Organization" or "Political Organization"
6. **Bio/Description:**
   ```
   The League of Women Voters of Newport County is a nonpartisan political 
   organization that encourages informed and active participation in government 
   across Newport, Jamestown, Little Compton, Middletown, Portsmouth, and 
   Tiverton, Rhode Island.
   
   • Register to vote
   • Attend candidate forums
   • Get involved in democracy
   
   Website: https://cball8475.github.io/lwv-newport-county/
   Email: LWVNewportCounty@gmail.com
   ```
7. **Click:** "Create Page"

#### Profile Picture:
- **Upload:** `LWVRI_rgb.png` or `logo-color.png` (from your files)

- **Make sure:** Logo is centered and clear

#### Cover Photo:
- **Upload:** The Capitol photo (people with "Voting is people power" signs)
- **File:** `image__3___1_.png` from your files
- **Size:** 820 x 312 pixels (Facebook will auto-crop)

## 📱 Facebook Page Settings (Optional)

### Messaging:
- Turn ON Messenger
- Set up automated greeting:
  ```
  Thanks for contacting LWV Newport County! We typically respond within 24 hours.
  
  Quick links:
  • Register to vote: https://vote.sos.ri.gov/
  • Our website: https://cball8475.github.io/lwv-newport-county/
  • Email: LWVNewportCounty@gmail.com
  ```

### Page Roles:
- Add board members as "Page Editors" or "Page Admins"
- They can help post content and respond to messages

### Notifications:
- Turn ON notifications for comments and messages
- Download Facebook Pages app for easy mobile posting
