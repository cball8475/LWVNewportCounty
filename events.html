"No Kings" National Day of Action

Saturday, March 28, 2026 • Statewide

The "No Kings" National Day of Action is scheduled for Saturday, March 28, 2026, featuring nationwide protests against authoritarianism and for democracy. Organizers are planning a large, peaceful demonstration, with rallies held at the Rhode Island State House in Providence. It is projected that there will also be demonstrations held in Newport, Jamestown, Portsmouth, and Tiverton.
