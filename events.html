Regular Programs

The League hosts regular meetings and events throughout the year. All members and interested community members are welcome.

Quarterly Member Meetings

Schedule: Announced in advance
Time: 5:30 PM

Regular gatherings for members and the public to discuss current issues and plan activities. Locations rotate around Newport County.

Board Meetings

Schedule: First Wednesday
Time: 1:00 PM

The Officers and Board of Directors meet to guide League activities. Members are welcome to observe these meetings.

2026 Candidate Forums

The League hosts nonpartisan candidate forums before every election. These forums give voters the opportunity to hear directly from candidates running for local and state office.

Forum dates will be announced as candidates declare their candidacy. All forums are free and open to the public, with questions submitted by community members and moderated by the League.
